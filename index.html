About

I transferred to UNC Chapel Hill from Rowan-Cabarrus Community College in 2023. Since then I've worked in three research labs spanning education technology and cognitive neuroscience, and I've been a UTA for four different courses. Outside of school I spent three and a half years running a robotics program at the Boys & Girls Club of Cabarrus County.

I'm interested in how we can use data to give instructors better insight into student learning, and how students interact with AI tools in educational settings. I care about doing this work ethically—my current projects involve IRB-approved protocols and FERPA-compliant data handling.
